To access an LNbits wallet from a mobile phone,
  1. Install Zeus;
  2. Go to Settings / Add a new node on Zeus.
  3. Select the desired wallet on this page;
  4. Scan one of the two QR codes from the mobile wallet.
  • Invoice URLs mean the mobile wallet will only have the authorization to read your payments and invoices and generate new invoices.
  • Admin URLs mean the mobile wallet will be able to pay invoices..
